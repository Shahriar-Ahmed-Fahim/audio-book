The Lost

In a quiet coastal town, a librarian discovers a forgotten ledger that maps the lives of its residents through the books they loved. As she follows the ledger's threads, she uncovers lost promises, hidden music, and a child's secret that could change everything. Each chapter gently peels back layers of memory, exploring love, grief, and the small acts of courage that stitch people together. With lyrical prose and vivid scenes, the novel invites readers to consider how stories shape identity and bind strangers into families.
